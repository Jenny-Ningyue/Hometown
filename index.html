Visit Nanjing

Enjoy the city with both ancient and modern styles.

Top three things to do at Nanjing

Come to the Confucius Temple and enjoy delicious food

The largest traditional ancient street market in China, where you can try variety of foods

Come to the Mausoleum of Sun Yat-sen and enjoy the view of nature

This is not only a good opportunity to learn more about Sun Yat-sen, but also a good chance to stay in nature. Take a deep breath here and feel the beauty of nature.

Come to the Nanjing Museum to know more about Nanjing history and culture

It cosists of six galleries in total. Especially notable is the museum's enormous collections of Ming and Qing imperial porcelai, which is among the largest in the world.
